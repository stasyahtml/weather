Zurich

Zurich is the largest city in Switzerland and has a reputation to match. Long known as the financial hub of Switzerland and boasting the world’s fourth biggest stock exchange, it is becoming quickly a very trend conscious city.
Located next to a picturesque lake and possessing a skyline broken up by church steeples and spectacular mountain scenery, Zurich is an arty and vibrant city.

Zermatt, a municipality in the district of Visp in Switzerland, is a wonderful holiday destination. It is home to an astonishing array of sights, activities and events.

Venice

Famed worldwide for its unique canals, beautiful architecture and carnevale, Venice is one of the most visited cities in the world.
